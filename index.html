PDF Bounding Box Viewer

Load a local PDF + AnchorGrid response JSON and visualize detections.

none

Supports full response payloads (`result.doors`, `result.toilets`, etc.) or any arrays of objects containing `bbox: {x1,y1,x2,y2}` and `page`.

How coordinates are detected

AnchorGrid detections are returned in PDF points. The model runs on a high-resolution internal render, then maps detections back to PDF space (`x1`, `y1`, `x2`, `y2`). This viewer projects those PDF points into screen pixels with `pdf.js` (`convertToViewportPoint`) and draws overlays at the transformed coordinates.
